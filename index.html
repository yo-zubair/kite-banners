Kite advertising is highly effective because it delivers unavoidable visibility and strong brand recall. People naturally look up, and moving visuals in the sky are far more memorable than static or digital ads.

Campaigns can be deployed in strategic coastal and high-traffic outdoor locations across the United States and internationally. Locations are selected based on audience, impressions, seasonality, and campaign goals.
